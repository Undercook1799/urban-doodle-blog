Using Anki to Study

Came across a flashcard studying app called Anki while I was looking through Reddit. I decided to check it out to see if it can help my with my College classes. So far, I enjoy it, I like the easy keyboard shortcuts usage for creating new cards and studying with them. However, for the decks part, I wish there was a folder button so that I can group the decks by subject. It will get a bit crazy if i have a lot of topics I want to try to memorize. Speaking of memorizing, there are four buttons to self press to see if you know the text: Again, Hard, Good, Easy. These are - to me at least - quite hard to do because I might have high standards for what is Good & Easy to remember.
